Press A Key to Start

  1. Simon will give you the first light signal. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color.

  2. Simon will choose another or the same color at random. Press the first color and then the second one chosen by Simon.

  3. Remember all the previous colours in order to increase the level. Once you failed one choice the game is over and you will start over again.
